Some Boiled Eggs

This is an egg that was boiled in a pot until it got all hard

Ingredients
  • An egg
  • A pot
  • Water
  • A fork (or your hands)
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps
  1. Put uncracked eggs in pot
  2. Set pot to high heat
  3. Wait 15 minutes for eggs to finish boiling
  4. CAREFULLY take eggs out of pot
  5. Remove their shells with a fork or hands
  6. Apply S&P if you want
  7. Cut them if you want
