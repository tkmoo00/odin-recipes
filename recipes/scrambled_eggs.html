Scrambled Eggs

A scrambled egg

This is an egg that was stirred in a pot until it was scrambled

Ingredients
  • An egg
  • A frying pan
  • Cooking spray
  • A fork
  • A bowl
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps
  1. Crack open egg into bowl
  2. Pour salt and pepper in bowl
  3. Stirr bowl until egg is all yellow
  4. pour cooking spray into frying pan
  5. heat up frying pan
  6. stir the egg yolk in frying pan until scrabmled
