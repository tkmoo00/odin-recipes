An Omelet

This is an egg that was stirred in a pot until it was flat enough to be flipped over something

Ingredients
  • An egg
  • A frying pan
  • Cooking spray
  • A fork
  • A bowl
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Something to flip egg over
Steps
  1. Crack open egg into bowl
  2. Pour salt and pepper in bowl
  3. Stirr bowl until egg is all yellow
  4. pour cooking spray into frying pan
  5. heat up frying pan
  6. wait for egg to harden enough to be flippable
  7. put stuff inside egg
  8. flip egg over stuff
